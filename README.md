# TournamentRepo

## Unified Sports Operations Platform

This app is designed to function as a single operating system for tournaments, leagues, camps, showcases, and multi-week programs.

### 1. Core Event Management Engine
- Automated scheduling for pool play, round robin, and single/double elimination with conflict detection, venue constraints, and drag-and-drop adjustments.
- Dynamic brackets with real-time updates, standings, and score propagation across devices.
- Multi-event support from one dashboard.
- Venue and resource management with court/field assignment, blackout dates, travel-distance logic, and time-between-games rules.

### 2. Registration, Rosters & Compliance
- Unified team and individual registration embedded on organizer websites.
- Roster management for coach invitations, adding/removing players, document tracking, and compliance.
- Waiver/document collection during registration, including medical forms and grade verification.

### 3. Identity, Age & Eligibility Verification
- Automated age verification using document scanning and secure multi-point checks.
- Facial recognition check-in for fast roster validation.
- Division eligibility enforcement for age/grade/skill criteria.
- Player safety controls to reduce mismatch-related injury risk.

### 4. Communication & Notifications
- Instant alerts for schedule changes, delays, gate updates, and emergencies.
- Team messaging for coaches, divisions, and staff groups.
- Real-time fan/parent updates for scores, directions, and announcements.

### 5. Ticketing & Access Control
- Digital ticketing with QR-code ticket presales and reduced cash handling.
- Gate check-in via QR scanning for attendees and players.
- Revenue tracking with automated reconciliation and reporting.

### 6. Live Scoring, Stats & Media
- Live score entry in real time.
- Auto-calculated standings, rankings, tiebreakers, and playoff seeding.
- Athlete highlight posting for clips, photos, and achievements.

### 7. Navigation & Event Discovery
- Event finder by sport, location, date, and organizer.
- Maps and turn-by-turn directions to venues and fields.
- Multi-event calendar for families managing multiple athletes.

### 8. Athlete Profiles & Recruiting Tools
- Verified athlete profiles with stats, academics, highlights, achievements, and badges.
- College coach access for athlete discovery, schedules, and event tracking.
- Exposure network/social feed for athletes, coaches, and fans.

### 9. Organizer Business Tools
- Financial dashboard for registrations, payments, ticket sales, and revenue share.
- Custom branding with white-label pages and mobile themes.
- Data export and integrations for rosters, schedules, and reports.

### 10. Mobile App Experience
- All-in-one app experience for schedules, rosters, tickets, highlights, messaging, and recruiting.
- Offline mode for schedule/roster access in low-connectivity environments.
- Personalized push notifications for teams, events, and athletes.

### 11. Social + Operational Layer
- Sports social feed for highlights, updates, and event content.
- Community interaction with likes, comments, tagging, and sharing.
- Content discovery for trending plays, top athletes, and event spotlights.

### 12. Admin Console
- Organizer dashboard for events, teams, payments, schedules, and compliance.
- Coach dashboard for rosters, messaging, schedules, and player tracking.
- Parent/athlete dashboard for personalized schedules, tickets, highlights, and notifications.

## Platform Summary

A single, vertically integrated youth sports operating system that:
- Runs tournaments, leagues, and camps
- Verifies athletes instantly
- Automates scheduling and brackets
- Handles registration, payments, and ticketing
- Connects athletes, parents, coaches, and fans
- Provides recruiting exposure
- Delivers a social layer for the sports ecosystem
